## Overview

Equipment represents persistent or semi-persistent tools that define a player’s **role, survivability, and information access**.

Inspired by XCOM-style utility systems, equipment provides:

* Limited slots
* Strong specialization
* Situational advantages

---

## Equipment Slots

Each player has:

* 1 base slot
* +1 conditional slot (role or progression)

Rules:

* No duplicate equipment
* Trade-offs are required

---

## Equipment Categories

### 1. Defensive Equipment

Improves survivability.

Examples:

* Reinforced clothing
* Antidote kit
* Body armor

Effects:

* Reduce kill chance
* Negate poison
* Survive one lethal attempt
